## Design Context

### Users
CPA exam candidates grinding through FAR, AUD, REG, BAR, ISC, and TCP sections. Typically 20–35 year olds with day jobs or recent graduates, studying 10–20 hrs/week over 6–18 months. They're under real pressure (expensive exams, 18-month expiry windows, career implications). They use the app daily — logging sessions, checking progress, competing socially. Mobile and desktop both.

### Brand Personality
Focused, motivating, ambitious. Think: serious athlete with a study plan, not a casual student. Not intimidating — but not soft either.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Light mode primary, dark mode supported
- Orange (#f97316) as primary accent — urgent, motivated energy
- Indigo (#5a5fcf) as secondary — discipline, structure
- Inter font (established in codebase — do not change globally)
- Clean and information-dense, not minimal. Data matters.

### Design Principles
1. **Hierarchy through space** — generous breathing around headings and section titles; tight grouping within sections
2. **Density with rhythm** — information-dense but with consistent spacing scale (4pt: 4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64)
3. **Left-aligned stats** — stat boxes read left, not centered — mirrors how athletes read performance data
4. **Active state clarity** — nav active states and selected states must be immediately obvious
5. **Progress is the hero** — numbers and streaks should be visually prominent; they are the emotional core of the app
