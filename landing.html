About Ledger

Built by candidates,
for candidates.

The CPA exam is one of the hardest professional credentialing exams in the world. A 50% first-attempt pass rate means half of every candidate who sits walks out defeated. Most study tools treat it like a content problem — more videos, more MCQs, more material. Ledger treats it like a discipline problem.

We built Ledger because the candidates who pass aren't necessarily the smartest — they're the most consistent. They show up every day, they track their hours, they know exactly where they stand, and they're surrounded by other people doing the same thing. Ledger gives you all of that in one place.

Our Mission
"To give every CPA candidate the structure, accountability, and community they need to pass all four sections — not someday, but on schedule."
