The Reality

Most candidates fail because they lose track — not because they aren't smart.

The CPA exam has a ~50% first-attempt pass rate across all four sections. The candidates who fail aren't unprepared — they run out of time, lose momentum, or have no way to tell if their current pace will get them through their exam window.

Ledger gives you real visibility: exactly how many hours you've put in, how many you need, and whether you're on track — per section, per week, every day.
