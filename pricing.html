FAQ

Common questions

Do I need a credit card for the free trial?
No. The 7-day free trial for Pro and Elite requires no payment information. You only enter billing details if you decide to keep your subscription after the trial ends.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel before the trial ends and you won't be charged. Cancel a paid subscription at any time — you keep access through the end of your billing period.
What's the difference between Pro and Elite?
Pro covers everything most candidates need — AI coaching, smart scheduling, advanced analytics, streak protection, and PDF reports. Elite adds the Score Predictor, AI Mnemonic Generator, Study Partner Pairing, and the full Exam Simulator. If you're not sure, start with Pro.
What is the 6-Month Cram Plan?
It's a one-time purchase (not a subscription) that gives you full Pro-tier access for 6 months, plus an exam-day protocol PDF and a personal milestone check-in. It's designed for candidates with a fixed exam window who want everything for less than 6 months of Pro billed monthly.
Does the annual plan auto-renew?
Yes, annual plans renew automatically unless cancelled. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings.
How does the student discount work?
Verify your student status with a .edu email address or proof of enrollment and receive 35% off the Pro plan ($13/mo instead of $20/mo). The discount applies for as long as your enrollment is active.
Can I switch plans later?
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.
